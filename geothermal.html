Geothermal Energy

CHANGE LATER

Geothermal energy is a renewable energy source that is environmentally friendly and can be used to generate electricity, heat, and light for homes, businesses, and industry.

Geothermal energy is harnessed using geothermal power plants that convert the heat from the Earth's core into mechanical power, which can be used to generate electricity.
